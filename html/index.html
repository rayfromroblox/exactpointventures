ABOUT US

What We Offer:

Welcome to Exact Point Ventures, where we redefine industrial commerce with innovation and reliability. We specialize in acquiring and redistributing a diverse range of assets, breathing new life into grounded and obsolete machinery while offering a curated selection of generators for uninterrupted power needs. Our fleet of shipping containers and trucks forms the backbone of countless supply chains, while our state-of-the-art cold storage equipment and rooms safeguard perishable goods.

What We Do:

At Exact Point Ventures, we contribute to sustainability efforts by recycling and repurposing general scrap metals. Join us in shaping a brighter tomorrow through our commitment to excellence and sustainability. Every asset tells a story of progress.


Our Mission:

At Exact Point Ventures, our mission is to redefine industrial commerce through innovation, reliability, and sustainability. We specialize in acquiring, redistributing, and repurposing assets to breathe new life into machinery and contribute to supply chains. We are committed to shaping a brighter tomorrow by safeguarding perishable goods and recycling scrap metals, ensuring every asset tells a story of progress.

DESIGNERS & INNOVATORS

At Exact-Point Ventures, we're more than just your go-to destination for affordable, high-quality second-hand goods in Kenya; we're also your trusted provider of steel and glass solutions. Rooted in our community, we've expanded our offerings to include a wide range of steel and glass products, from sturdy construction materials to elegant home decor pieces. Whether you're renovating your space, upgrading your infrastructure, or adding a touch of sophistication to your surroundings, you can count on us to deliver top-notch steel and glass solutions that combine durability, style, and affordability. Welcome to Exact-Point Ventures, where quality, variety, and value meet in every steel beam and glass pane.
